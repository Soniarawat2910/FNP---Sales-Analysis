# 📊 FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built in Microsoft Excel using real-world business logic. It analyzes sales performance across different dimensions such as **occasions, categories, cities, time, and products**.

The dashboard is interactive and designed to help stakeholders quickly understand key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The main objectives of this project are:

* To analyze sales data and extract meaningful insights
* To track key business KPIs like revenue, orders, and customer spending
* To identify top-performing categories, products, and cities
* To understand seasonal and time-based sales trends
* To build an interactive and user-friendly dashboard using Excel

---

## 📁 Dataset Description

Here using 2 CSV FILES 
* customer file 
* products file
  
The dataset includes the following fields:

* Order Date
* Delivery Date
* City
* Occasion (Festival/Event)
* Product Category
* Product Name
* Revenue (Sales Amount)
* Order Details

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🔹 Revenue by Occasion

* Highest revenue generated during **Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan**
* Lower sales observed during **Diwali and Valentine’s Day**

---

### 🔹 Revenue by Category

* **Colors and Soft Toys** are top-performing categories
* **Mugs and Plants** show relatively lower sales

---

### 🔹 Monthly Trends

* Sales peak during **February and August**
* Significant drop observed in mid-year months

---

### 🔹 Top Products

* Products like **Magnam Set and Quia Gift** generate highest revenue
* Indicates strong demand for premium gift items

---

### 🔹 Top Cities by Orders

* Cities like **Imphal, Kavali, and Dhanbad** lead in total orders
* Shows demand is not limited to metro cities

---

### 🔹 Revenue by Hour

* Peak sales occur during **afternoon and evening hours**
* Suggests customers are more active post-working hours

## 📌 Conclusion

* Seasonal events play a major role in driving sales
* Certain product categories consistently outperform others
* Sales are influenced by time (hour & month trends)
* Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities contribute significantly to orders
* Dashboard enables quick and effective business decision-making
